E.D.SMITH

  • Goal: Create a community of engaged customers
  • Solution: Developed millennial-targeted creative and tailored cross-channel campaign sites and brand social channels.
  • Role: Creative Direction, Lead Design, QA Lead, Experience Designer

Starting and shaping the TasteCretor Community has been a fulfilling experience. Telling E.D.SMITH’s 133 year old Canadian story to the social world.

When we started our 3-year relationship with E.D.SMITH they had an out of date web site and no social presence. We built and told the E.D.SMITH story with the creation of social channels, new website and seasonal cross-channel campaigns.

We cultivated and branded the new online community TasteCreator. The Community grew into the new face for the brand.

The Plan

Target the millennial demographic, cultivate and create campaigns with creative and rewards that they care about.

  • Monthly Newsletter
  • Facebook Photo contests
  • Seasonal sweepstakes & giveaways
  • Google Adwords
  • Facebook AdManager

The Cross-Channel Campaigns

The campaigns aimed to raise awareness of E.D.SMITH brand and products, increase targeted to grow engagement on Facebook, Instagram and Email while acquiring new consumer who may not have heard about the brand. Slowly we put together a Consumer database of consumer profiles. Below is the basic flow to over 14 seasonal campaigns we ran promoting the Tastcreator community and E.D.SMITH products and recipes.

#SummerFun

The #SummerFun campaign was centered around kicking-off summer the E.D.SMITH way. Customers were engaged to enter the sweepstakes through compelling visuals and messaging that hit on the ideas of summer fun, vacations, picnics and being out by the sun. On the social media front, customers were asked to share their own summer experiences via comments on posts.
