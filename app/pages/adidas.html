adidas

  • Goal: Adidas wanted to identify people buying team product and were socially vocal about it. Grouping these "Engaged Fan Consumers" with their teams online and offline provided a real metric for future adidas or partners marketing.

  • Solution: Our team created campaigns to reward the offline to online interaction through sweepstakes, contests, and giveaways. We identified which customers purchased in store and engaged with online adidas campaigns.

  • Role: Lead Design, Front End Development, User Experience, Quality Assurance

The Plan

To attract these consumer fans, we used retailers and team's large social networks to promote and attract participants. We developed photo and video contest sites, awarding winners with VIP experiences, weekly ticket giveaways, and signed adidas team merchandise.

We created content calendars and media collateral for sports teams, players, and retailers to support the lifetime of our campaign site. All the entries passed through a form page that often used social authentication to make the entries easier for users.

The Tagga platform mapped and grouped the user profiles for the client.

Below, demonstrates the user flow used in these campaigns. Online and offline channels had unique URLs, keywords, or hashtags, that lead the consumer to a landing page, where they entered the campaign. After there was always exclusive content, coupons, and a retailer locator.
