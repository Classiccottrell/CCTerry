UI/UX Design


The data from each client campaign is connected to our Consumer Data Platform, which creates a complete, 360° consumer profile out of raw data, through mapping and custom data modals.

I am responsible for the development and maintaining of the software UI/UX by adding new features to the CDP through wireframes, designing and building prototypes, to create a visual road map for our front end developer.
