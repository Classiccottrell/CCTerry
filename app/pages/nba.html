#NBASwingman

NBA and adidas wanted to boost sales and discover the true market of the swingman jersey product line.

Using the Swingman commercials, we launched the video contest, "How Swingman are you?". Users signed up and submitted a video using the campaign hashtag on their social channel of choice (Instagram or Vine) from their mobile device. The winner received 2 all inclusive tickets to the NBA Finals.
