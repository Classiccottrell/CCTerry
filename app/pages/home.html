E.D.SMITH


Problem: How do we attract the millennial audience Solution: Developed millennial-targeted creative through tailored cross-channel campaign sites and content strategies. Role: Creative Direction, Lead Design, QA Lead, Experience Designer

Working alongside a client who was new to the digital world allowed for a clean slate and a place to implement and grow my creative process.

When we started our 3-year relationship with E.D.SMITH they had no online marketing. We built and told the E.D.SMITH story with social channels and website we created, that resulted in a new face for the brand.

The Plan

Tell the E.D.SMITH Story

Appeal to the millennial demographic, cultivate and create a customer data platform for marketing.

Over our 3-year relationship, we developed a facebook page website, Instagram account and email newsletter list of over 25,000 consumers. We were able to design and direct creative for this client shaping a new face for the brand. creating sweepstakes micro campaigns launch on targeted channels to encourage overall brand awareness and brand loyalty.

#SummerFun

Role: Art Direction, Lead design, front End Developement, campaign stratagy

The #SummerFun campaign was centered around the E.D.SMITH Branded Family and kicking-off summer the E.D.SMITH way. Customers were engaged to enter the sweepstakes through compelling visuals and messaging that hit on the ideas of summer fun, vacations, picnics and being out by the sun. On the social media front, customers were asked to share their own summer experiences via comments on posts.

The goals of this campaign were to raise awareness of E.D.SMITH branded products, increase engagement on Facebook and acquire new consumer email addresses to build E.D.SMITH’s subscriber list.

Pack a Lunch With Triple Fruits

Role: Art Direction, Lead design, front End Developement, campaign stratagy
