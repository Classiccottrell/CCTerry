#NBASwingman

NBA and adidas wanted to boost sales and discover the true market of the swingman jersey product line.

We created a cross-channel campaign that was deployed in-store and across digital channels. Leveraging the wildly popular Swingman commercials we launched a video contest, "How Swingman are you?" users signed up and then submit a video using the hashtag on their social channel of choice (Instagram orVine) from their mobile device. The winner received 2 All-inclusive tickets to the NBA Finals.
