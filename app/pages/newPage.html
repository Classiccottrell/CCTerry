E.D.SMITH - #SummerFun

Campaing Run Dates: July 17,2015 - July 31.2015

The #SummerFun campaign was centered around the E.D.SMITH Branded Family and kicking-off summer the E.D.SMITH way. Customers were engaged to enter the sweepstakes through compelling visuals and messaging that hit on the ideas of summer fun, vacations, picnics and being out by the sun. On the social media front, customers were asked to share their own summer experiences via comments on posts.

The goals of this campaign were to raise awareness of E.D.SMITH branded products, increase engagement on Facebook and acquire new consumer email addresses to build E.D.SMITH’s subscriber list.

Kicking Hours - 1 Year Free Ground Coffee

Campaing Run Dates: June 10,2015 - July 7,2015

The #KickAssGround sweepstakes was aimed at promoting Kicking Horse Coffee ground products, a convenient yet high quality, pre-ground option for busy coffee-lovers. Consumers were given the chance to win weekly prizes as well as a grand prize, which consisted of a year’s supply of Kick Ass ground coffee (52 bags delivered in 12 monthly shipments).

To enter the sweepstakes, consumers had to provide their name, email and (optionally) subscribe to Kicking Horse Coffee’s email subscriber base. Consumers also had the option to share the promotion with their friends and family via social media once signed-up.
