#SummerFun

The #SummerFun campaign was centered around kicking off summer the E.D.SMITH way. Customers were engaged through a sweepstakes contest, compelling visuals and messaging that hit on the ideas of summer fun, vacations, picnics, and being out in the sun. On the social media front, customers were asked to share their own summer experiences via comments on posts.

Pack a Lunch With Triple Fruits

I was heavily involved in the conception and design of the key photo used in the key visual design. I worked closely with the recipe photography team to give direction on the layout of items used in the photo.

In this campaign, we focused on the Triple Fruits products and recipes. We concepted key recipes and photography with the recipe photography team, to offer creativity for customer's back to school routines.
